About LGU Citizen Portal

Empowering communities through transparent and efficient road infrastructure monitoring and management

Our Mission

To provide citizens with a user-friendly platform for reporting, tracking, and monitoring road infrastructure issues, ensuring faster response times and improved public service delivery through technology-driven solutions.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital platform for citizen-government collaboration in infrastructure management, fostering transparency, accountability, and community participation in local governance.

What We Do

The LGU Citizen Portal serves as a comprehensive solution for road and infrastructure monitoring, connecting citizens directly with local government services.

Key Features

  • Real-time Reporting: Citizens can report road damage, potholes, and other infrastructure issues instantly
  • Track Progress: Monitor the status of your reports from submission to resolution
  • Photo Documentation: Upload images and videos to provide detailed evidence of issues
  • GPS Location: Automatic location tagging for accurate problem identification
  • Priority System: Issues are categorized by severity for efficient resource allocation
  • Transparent Updates: Regular status updates on ongoing infrastructure projects

How It Works

Step 1: Report an Issue

Citizens can easily report infrastructure problems through our mobile-friendly web portal. Simply describe the issue, upload photos, and the system automatically captures the location.

Step 2: Verification Process

Our team reviews and verifies each report, categorizing issues by severity and urgency. Critical issues are prioritized for immediate attention.

Step 3: Assignment & Action

Verified reports are assigned to the appropriate LGU departments. Resources are allocated based on priority and availability.

Step 4: Resolution & Feedback

Once the issue is resolved, citizens receive notifications and can provide feedback on the quality of service received.

Benefits for Citizens

  • Convenience: Report issues anytime, anywhere using your smartphone or computer
  • Transparency: Track the progress of your reports in real-time
  • Accountability: Hold government agencies accountable for infrastructure maintenance
  • Community Impact: Contribute to improving your neighborhood's infrastructure
  • Faster Response: Quicker resolution of infrastructure problems

Benefits for Government

  • Efficient Resource Management: Optimize allocation of maintenance resources
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Use analytics to identify infrastructure patterns and needs
  • Improved Service Delivery: Faster response times and higher resolution rates
  • Cost Savings: Preventive maintenance reduces long-term repair costs
  • Public Trust: Build stronger relationships with the community

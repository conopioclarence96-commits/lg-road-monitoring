‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? +
Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Create Account" button on the login page, fill in your personal information including your email address and phone number, and choose a secure password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. Once verified, you can start reporting infrastructure issues immediately.
What types of issues can I report? +
You can report various infrastructure issues including: potholes, road cracks, damaged street lights, flooding problems, broken traffic signs, debris on roads, bridge damage, drainage issues, and any other road or infrastructure-related problems. Each report is categorized to ensure it reaches the right department.
How long does it take to resolve reported issues? +
Resolution time varies depending on the severity and type of issue. Emergency issues are typically addressed within 24 hours, while standard repairs may take 3-7 business days. Major infrastructure projects may take longer. You can track the status of your report in real-time through your dashboard.
Do I need to provide photos when reporting? +
While photos are not mandatory, they are highly recommended as they help our teams better understand and prioritize issues. Clear photos can significantly speed up the verification process and ensure the right resources are allocated. You can upload up to 5 photos per report.
How do I track the status of my report? +
After logging in, go to your dashboard where you'll see all your reported issues. Each report shows its current status (Pending, Under Review, In Progress, Resolved). You'll also receive email notifications when there are updates to your reports. Click on any report to view detailed information and timeline.
Can I report anonymously? +
Yes, you can choose to report issues anonymously. However, creating an account provides benefits like tracking your reports, receiving updates, and providing additional information if needed. Anonymous reports are still processed with the same priority as regular reports.
What happens after I submit a report? +
Once submitted, your report goes through a verification process where our team reviews the details and assigns it to the appropriate department. You'll receive a confirmation with a reference number. The report is then categorized by priority and scheduled for action based on urgency and resource availability.
How do I contact support for technical issues? +
For technical support, you can email us at support@lgu.gov.ph, call our hotline at 1-800-LGU-HELP, or use the live chat feature on our website. Our support team is available Monday-Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM. For urgent issues outside these hours, please call our emergency hotline.

Common Issues and Solutions

I can't log in to my account +
First, ensure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" to reset it. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again. If issues persist, contact our support team for assistance.
Photos won't upload +
Check that your photos are in supported formats (JPG, PNG) and under 10MB in size. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try refreshing the page and attempting the upload again. If using a mobile device, make sure you have granted the app permission to access your photos.
Location services aren't working +
Make sure location services are enabled in your device settings and browser permissions. Try refreshing the page and allowing location access when prompted. You can also manually enter the address if automatic location detection fails.

üì± Mobile App Help

Using our mobile app? Here are some app-specific tips:

How do I download the mobile app? +
Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. Search for "LGU Citizen Portal" in the App Store or Google Play Store, or visit our website and click the download links. The app is free to download and use.
Enable push notifications +
To receive updates about your reports, enable push notifications in your device settings. Go to Settings > Notifications > LGU Citizen Portal and toggle notifications on. This ensures you never miss important updates about your reported issues.
